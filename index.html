About Me

Creative and detail-oriented Video Editor specializing in producing engaging content for social media, marketing campaigns, and YouTube. Skilled in storytelling, color grading, audio syncing, and motion graphics to transform raw footage into polished videos. Adept at creating reels, ads, and promotional content while ensuring alignment with brand guidelines. Passionate about delivering impactful visual stories, thriving in collaborative teams, and meeting deadlines across multiple projects.

With experience at Talent Catalyst AI as a Freelance Video Editor and previously at DebugShala, Indore, I've developed expertise in creating promotional videos, ad creatives, and branded content tailored for digital marketing campaigns. My work focuses on driving engagement, conversions, and brand awareness through platform-optimized video content.

I specialize in trend-driven content creation to boost virality and connect with target audiences. My technical skills include using AI tools like Runway ML, Nano Banana, Kapwing AI, and more for auto-captioning, video upscaling, background removal, and idea generation to enhance workflows.
